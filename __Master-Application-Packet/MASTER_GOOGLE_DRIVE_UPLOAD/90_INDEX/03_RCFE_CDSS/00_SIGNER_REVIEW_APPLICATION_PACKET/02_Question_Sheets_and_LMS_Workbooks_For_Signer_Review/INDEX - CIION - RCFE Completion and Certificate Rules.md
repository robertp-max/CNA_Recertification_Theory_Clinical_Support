# RCFE Completion and Certificate Rules

Status: Draft / Pending CDSS/ACS Approval

No completion or certificate may issue unless all filed-duration, participation, assessment, evaluation, and approval-metadata gates are satisfied.

## Global Certificate Block

Certificates remain disabled while any of the following are missing:

- Full approved duration requirement is not met.
- Active participation checks are incomplete.
- Post-test is not passed at 80 percent or better.
- Required course evaluation is incomplete.
- CDSS/ACS approval, vendor number, course number, and authorized certificate metadata are not available.
- Instructor evidence attachment/currentness has not been verified.

## Certificate Wording Control

Before approval, any internal test certificate must be watermarked `NOT VALID FOR CE CREDIT`. After approval, certificate text must use only issued vendor/course metadata and must not imply approval beyond the approved course, date, format, and hours.

## Retention

Retain completion, grade, participation, evaluation, attendance, certificate, and approval-metadata evidence with the course file according to the approved recordkeeping policy and at the records address: 419 E. Hamilton Ave., Campbell, CA 95008.
