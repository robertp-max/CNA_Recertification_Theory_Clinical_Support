RCFE Administrator CE requirements

For RCFE Administrator recertification, Title 22 section 87407 requires at least 40 classroom hours of continuing education in each two-year certification period, including at least 8 hours related to Alzheimer’s disease and other dementias and at least 4 hours in laws, regulations, policies, and procedural standards. It also states continuing education must be related to the Core of Knowledge and current/relevant to facility operations and care.

The same regulation says up to one-half of the required 40 hours may be satisfied through interactive online courses, the Department will not count more than 10 hours of instruction in a single day, and home study/correspondence courses do not count because they are not interactive by design.

BRN RN Case Management CE requirements

For BRN, the controlling term is contact hours. The BRN states California RNs must complete 30 contact hours of continuing education every two years and that courses must be taken through a CE provider recognized by the Board. BRN also states acceptable content must be relevant to nursing practice, above entry-level licensure knowledge, and related to scientific knowledge, technical skills, or direct/indirect patient/client care.

BRN also makes an important distinction: the Board approves Continuing Education Providers, but that approval does not constitute endorsement of course content.
