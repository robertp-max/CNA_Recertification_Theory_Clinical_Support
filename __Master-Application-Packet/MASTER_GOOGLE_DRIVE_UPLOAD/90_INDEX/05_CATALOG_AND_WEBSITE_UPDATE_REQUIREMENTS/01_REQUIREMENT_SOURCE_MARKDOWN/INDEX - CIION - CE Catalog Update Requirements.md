Status: Draft / Pending Approval

## Purpose

This source Markdown supports the public PDF `CIION - CE Catalog Update Requirements.pdf`. It defines catalog update requirements for the CI Institute of Nursing continuing education application tracks and preserves the approval-safe posture required before public release.

## Program-Specific Requirements

### CNA Renewal Online CE

- Describe 24 online CE hours.
- State online CE only.
- Do not describe clinical hours.
- Do not claim full 48-hour CNA renewal completion.
- Block certificate release until CDPH approval and NAC#/course metadata exist.

### RCFE Administrator Continuing Education

- Describe 9 courses / 27 hours.
- Preserve Draft / Pending CDSS/ACS Approval.
- Do not represent the future 40-hour / 12-module concept as current.
- Block certificate release until approval and course/vendor metadata exist.

### RN Case Management Continuing Education

- Describe 30 contact hours.
- Use contact hours, not CEUs, except in prohibited-language context.
- Do not claim certification, credentialing, licensure expansion, or scope expansion.
- Block certificate release until BRN CEP approval and provider/course metadata exist.

## Records, Privacy, Accessibility, and Support

- Retain completion, quiz, evaluation, attestation, review, and certificate-release evidence according to the data retention, backup, and security specification.
- Do not request, collect, or expose patient identifiers or private clinical details.
- Provide readable catalog language, keyboard and mobile usability expectations, support routing, and accessibility issue escalation.
- Explain supported access methods and how learners or reviewers request help.
