# 20 - Post-Approval Activation Plan

Status: Draft / Pending Approval. Execute only after a lane's regulator approval is granted.

## 1. Update approval numbers
- Enter the regulator-issued approval/provider number(s) and effective/expiration dates into the certificate approval metadata fields (per lane). (Regulator-issued - final human verification required.)

## 2. Update certificate wording
- Set certificate text to match approved terms exactly: CNA (online CE hours; no clinical hours), RCFE (approved course titles/hours), BRN (30 contact hours; contact hours not CEUs; no certification/scope claim).

## 3. Enable certificate release
- Set the approval flag and enable the certificate activity; verify release gates (completion + passing assessment + attestation + approval metadata). Re-run CT-02.

## 4. Activate public/catalog language
- Replace "Draft / Pending Approval" / "Pending BRN CEP Approval" labels with approved status wording. Ensure no overclaim (no clinical hours for CNA; contact hours for BRN; RCFE filed structure).

## 5. Confirm reviewer credentials
- Re-validate any standing reviewer/compliance access; rotate or deactivate temporary credentials as appropriate.

## 6. Preserve pre-approval audit
- Archive the pre-approval evidence (build state, QA results, that no certificate was issued pre-approval). Do not delete prior logs.

## 7. Lock approved version
- Snapshot/lock the approved course version; restrict edits to change-controlled updates with documentation.

## 8. Backup approved course
- Create and store a course backup (.mbz) of the approved version, with offsite copy.

## 9. Train admin/support staff
- Brief Admin/Manager/Support staff on the activated certificate process, override logging, no-PHI handling, and reviewer provisioning. Record training completion.
