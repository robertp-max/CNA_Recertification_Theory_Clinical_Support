## Active Participation Requirement

Each learner must complete at least one active interaction in every unit. Acceptable evidence is H5P completion, Lesson branch completion, submitted reflection, quiz attempt, or Feedback attestation tied to the unit. Passive page views alone are not sufficient for unit completion.

## Final Assessment

Create a final assessment after U12 using a random or fixed subset from the 120-question bank. Recommended configuration: 50 questions, coverage across all 12 units, 80 percent pass, shuffled questions and answers, limited attempts per approved local policy, deferred feedback, and restricted answer review.

## Certificate Gate

Do not issue a certificate unless all of the following are true:

- Required unit activities U01-U12 complete.
- Active participation/interaction complete in every unit.
- Unit quizzes and final assessment passed at the configured threshold.
- Identity and learner attestation statement complete.
- No-PHI agreement complete.
- Approval status allows certificate issuance.
- Provider/course identifiers and certificate wording are confirmed.

Because this packet is Draft / Pending CDPH Approval, certificate issuance remains disabled until approval status is updated by authorized administration.

## Explicit Exclusions

- Do not count clinical or simulation time toward online CE.
- Do not include clinical hours in certificate logic.
- Do not claim CDPH approval unless approval has been issued.
- Do not copy government forms or signer binders into Moodle.
- Do not use old ContentV2 M00-M07 or an 8 x 3 hour structure as the current course.
