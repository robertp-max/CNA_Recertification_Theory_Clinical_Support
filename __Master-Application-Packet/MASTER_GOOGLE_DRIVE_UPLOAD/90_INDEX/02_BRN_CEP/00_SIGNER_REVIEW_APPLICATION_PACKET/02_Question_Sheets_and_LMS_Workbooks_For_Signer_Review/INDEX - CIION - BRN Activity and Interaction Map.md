## Contact-Hour Support

Each module is built to reasonably support 3 contact hours through a combination of lesson content, case application, active learner decision-making, documentation practice, knowledge check assessment, and reflection. The total target across 10 modules is 30 contact hours.

## Moodle Evidence Summary

- Activity completion logs confirm viewed pages and completed activity blocks.
- H5P or Lesson reports confirm active scenario participation.
- Assignment records retain documentation/application exercises.
- Quiz reports retain attempts, scores, answers, and pass status.
- Feedback or attestation records retain learner reflection and independent-completion attestation.
- Course completion records support certificate-release review after approval metadata is available.
