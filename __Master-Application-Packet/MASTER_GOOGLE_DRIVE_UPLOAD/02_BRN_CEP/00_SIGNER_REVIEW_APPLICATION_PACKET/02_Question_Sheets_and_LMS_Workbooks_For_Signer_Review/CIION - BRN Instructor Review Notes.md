# BRN Instructor Review Notes

Status: Draft / Pending BRN CEP Approval

These notes are for instructor/admin review before Moodle build, pilot testing, and any future approval-sensitive publication decision.

## Review Priorities

- Confirm that each module remains relevant to RN practice and direct or indirect patient/client care.
- Confirm that learning objectives are behavioral learner objectives, not instructor goals.
- Confirm that scenarios and documentation exercises reflect RN case management scope and do not ask learners to make provider-only decisions.
- Confirm that quiz questions test applied RN case management judgment, documentation, communication, patient advocacy, and compliance guardrails.
- Confirm that all learner-facing materials use contact hours as the primary measurement.
- Confirm that no approval claim, provider number, or certificate statement is enabled until BRN CEP approval is issued.

## Pending Provider Metadata

Do not populate certificate or approval fields until the provider approval record is available. Required metadata may include provider/business name, issued BRN provider number, approved provider statement, responsible person, recordkeeping location, course date rules, instructor information, and certificate template approval by compliance.

## Moodle Build QA

- Confirm 10 module shells exist.
- Confirm each module shows 3 contact hours.
- Confirm total course target is 30 contact hours.
- Confirm each module contains overview, lesson, scenario, assignment, quiz, reflection/attestation, and completion gate.
- Confirm 150 or more question-bank rows are available.
- Confirm all quizzes are mapped to the correct module ID.
- Confirm no government forms or signer binders are copied into the course-content folder.
- Confirm certificate activity remains hidden or blocked until all gates are met.

## Approval and Advertising Guardrails

- Use Draft / Pending BRN CEP Approval in internal build notes.
- Do not advertise BRN approval before approval is issued.
- Do not issue RN continuing education certificates before provider approval metadata is available and completion evidence is confirmed.
- Do not call contact hours CEUs as the primary label. The course is measured in contact hours.
