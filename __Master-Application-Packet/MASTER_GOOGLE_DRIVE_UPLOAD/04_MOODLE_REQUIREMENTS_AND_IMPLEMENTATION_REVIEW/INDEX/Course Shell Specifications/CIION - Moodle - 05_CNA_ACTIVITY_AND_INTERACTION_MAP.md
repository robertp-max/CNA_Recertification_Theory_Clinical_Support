## Interaction Standards

- Each unit must require a learner decision, sort, branch, rewrite, or reflection tied to a fictional scenario.
- Each interaction must reinforce CNA scope: observe, assist, report to licensed nurse, document within scope, protect dignity and privacy.
- Each free-text activity must display a no-PHI warning.
- Interactions may be implemented as H5P, Moodle Lesson, Quiz, Assignment, or Feedback based on local Moodle build capacity.
