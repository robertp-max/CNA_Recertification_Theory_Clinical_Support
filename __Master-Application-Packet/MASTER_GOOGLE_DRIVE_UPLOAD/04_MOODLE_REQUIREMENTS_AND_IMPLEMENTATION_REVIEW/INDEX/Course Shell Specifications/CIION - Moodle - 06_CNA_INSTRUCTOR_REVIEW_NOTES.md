## Instructor Monitoring Notes

Review completion reports weekly during pilot. Investigate learners who pass quizzes without completing interactions or whose time logs are inconsistent with the 2-hour target. Audit free-text reflections for accidental PHI and remove/secure any submission that includes real resident or facility information according to privacy policy.
