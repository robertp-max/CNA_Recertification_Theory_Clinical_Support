# BRN Policies Packet

## Refund / nonattendance policy draft

The provider will honor written cancellation through the first class session or the seventh calendar day after enrollment, whichever is later. After the cancellation period, any eligible refund will follow a pro-rata method when 60 percent or less of the scheduled instructional period has been completed. Refunds are to be issued within 45 days after valid cancellation or withdrawal notice is received.

## Cancellation notification policy draft

Cancellation may be given in writing by mail, email, or hand delivery to the provider's designated records/contact address. A mailed notice is effective when deposited in the mail with proper address and postage. The learner may use any written notice that clearly states the intent to cancel.

## Refund timeline policy draft

Any refund due after cancellation or withdrawal will be processed within 45 days after the provider receives valid written notice or determines the effective withdrawal date under the provider's attendance records.

## Record-retention policy draft

The provider will maintain required continuing-education records for not less than four years. Institutional source policies already maintain student records for five years from the last day of attendance, completion, or withdrawal, with transcripts retained indefinitely. BRN-facing records will therefore be retained for at least the required four-year minimum at 419 E. Hamilton Ave., Campbell, CA 95008.

## Proof-of-attendance / certificate issuance policy draft

No blank certificate will be issued. A certificate of completion will be issued only after the provider confirms attendance/completion requirements and final evaluation/attestation requirements have been satisfied. The participant will be instructed to retain proof of attendance for four years.
