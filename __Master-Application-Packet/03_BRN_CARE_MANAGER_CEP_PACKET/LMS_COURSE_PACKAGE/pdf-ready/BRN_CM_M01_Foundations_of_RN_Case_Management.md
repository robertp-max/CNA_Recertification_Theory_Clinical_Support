### BRN Relevance Rationale

Establishes the RN scope-of-practice and role boundaries that make nurse-led case management safe and lawful, framing case management as a function within registered-nursing practice rather than a separate license.

### Module Description

Orients the RN to the definition, purpose, and process of nurse-led case management; differentiates it from social work and unlicensed coordination; and fixes the scope boundaries and escalation duties that govern every later module.

### Measurable Learning Objectives

By the end of this module the learner will be able to:

1. Define RN case management and distinguish it from social work and unlicensed care coordination using BRN scope language.
2. Identify three RN scope-of-practice boundaries that constrain case-management decisions.
3. Describe how care coordination qualifies as direct/indirect patient care under nursing practice.
4. Recognize when a case-management task must be escalated to a licensed prescriber or RN supervisor.

### Required Readings / Learning Content Sections

- Reading 1.1 - Definition and purpose of RN case management.

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Case management | A collaborative process of assessment, planning, coordination, and monitoring of services to meet a patient's health needs. |
| Scope of practice | The activities a licensed RN is authorized to perform under state law and competency. |
| Escalation | Routing a decision or task to a prescriber/RN supervisor when it exceeds the case manager's role. |
| Indirect patient care | Nursing activities that support patient outcomes without hands-on care (e.g., coordination, documentation). |
| Care coordination | Organizing patient care activities and sharing information among participants to achieve safer, more effective care. |

### Fictional / De-identified Case Scenario

*All clinical content is fictional and de-identified. No PHI.* Nurse R receives a home-health referral for a fictional 72-year-old client recovering from a hip procedure. The referral asks Nurse R to 'adjust the pain medication.' No prescriber order accompanies the request.

### Learner Activities

### Documentation / Decision-Making Exercise

Write a 5-line scope-and-escalation note documenting why the requested medication change is outside RN case-management scope, what you did instead, and to whom the request was routed.
