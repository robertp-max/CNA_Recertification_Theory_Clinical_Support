# READ ME FIRST — CI Institute of Nursing Signer / Reviewer Packet

**Status: Draft / Pending Approval — Signer & Reviewer Review Version**

## What this folder is

This is the clean, signer-friendly, Google Drive–ready version of the CI Institute of Nursing
regulatory application packet. It contains only the final review and submission materials for the
three approval lanes:

- **01_CNA_CDPH_CE** — California CNA recertification online CE (CDPH).
- **02_RCFE_CDSS_CETP** — RCFE Continuing Education Training Program vendor & course approval (CDSS).
- **03_BRN_CARE_MANAGER_CEP** — BRN Continuing Education Provider (CEP) application.

It is **not** the full source archive. Internal drafts, build scripts, research files, audit
fragments, generated outputs, one-page branded fragments, and superseded materials have been
deliberately excluded. See `99_NOT_FOR_SUBMISSION_REFERENCE/00_EXCLUDED_FILES_NOT_FOR_SUBMISSION.md`.

## Who should review it

- The **authorized signer** (Maria Divina Bustos, CEO / Authorized Representative).
- The **SME / instructor of record** (Vanessa Valerio, RN) for instructor evidence items.
- Any **legal / privacy reviewer** confirming notice and attestation language.
- The **LMS administrator** confirming reviewer access for the regulator.

## What must be signed

- **CNA:** CDPH 192B and CDPH 193 — authorized-signer signature, printed name, and date.
- **RCFE:** LIC 9141 vendor application and the nine LIC 9140 course-approval forms — authorized signer.
- **BRN:** BRN CEP application — authorized signer / provider responsible person.
- Each lane's **Final Verification Checklist** lists the exact signature and attestation lines.

## What must be attached

- **Instructor / SME evidence:** resume / CV, active license verification, and license expiration
  confirmation (Vanessa Valerio).
- **Business / identity records** as listed in each lane checklist (e.g., business license, catalog,
  reviewer-access credentials / LMS URL, user ID and password).
- The combined official forms in each lane folder are the forms to attach to the regulator submission.

## What must be paid

- **CNA (CDPH 192B):** No fee required.
- **RCFE (CDSS CETP):** Vendor fee (LIC 9141) **$140** + course fees **$270** = **$410 total**.
  See `02_RCFE_CDSS_CETP/04_RCFE_FEE_NOTICES_READY_FOR_PAYMENT.pdf`.
- **BRN (CEP):** Confirm current BRN CEP application fee on the BRN fee schedule at submission time.

## What must NOT be submitted

- The design **template** / branding source file (`TEMPLATE MUST USE.pdf`).
- Any internal build scripts, research, QA images, `BRANDED_PDF_OUTPUTS`, `__pycache__`, or
  draft / reference-only / superseded fragments.
- Anything listed in `99_NOT_FOR_SUBMISSION_REFERENCE/00_EXCLUDED_FILES_NOT_FOR_SUBMISSION.md`.
