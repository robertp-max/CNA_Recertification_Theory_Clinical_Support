# BRN Completion and Certificate Rules

Status: Draft / Pending BRN CEP Approval

Primary measurement: contact hours.

This document defines Moodle completion, final assessment, and certificate-release rules for the 30 contact-hour BRN RN Case Management / Care Manager course-content package.

## Course Completion Rule

A learner may be marked complete only after all of the following are true:

- All 10 module overview pages are viewed.
- All 10 module lesson-content pages are viewed.
- All 10 case scenario activities are completed.
- All required active learner interactions are completed.
- All 10 documentation/application exercises are submitted.
- All 10 module knowledge check quizzes are passed according to provider policy.
- The M10 integrated capstone packet is submitted and accepted.
- The final assessment is passed according to provider policy.
- The learner reflection/attestation is submitted for every module.
- BRN provider approval metadata is available before certificate release.

## Module Quiz Rule

Each module includes at least 15 multiple-choice questions in the CSV question bank. Recommended configuration: 15 questions per module, 80 percent passing score, randomized answer order, feedback shown after submission, and retakes controlled by provider policy.

## Final Capstone and Final Assessment Structure

The final assessment is located in M10 and should include:

- Integrated capstone scenario completion.
- Capstone documentation packet submission.
- Final multiple-choice assessment using M10 questions and cumulative items selected from M01 through M09.
- Instructor or admin review of capstone documentation for RN scope, patient-care relevance, factual charting, coordination accuracy, and appropriate escalation.
- Learner attestation confirming independent completion.

## Certificate Release Gate

Do not issue or unlock a certificate unless all of the following conditions are satisfied:

- all module activities completed
- required interactions completed
- quizzes/final assessment passed
- learner attestation completed
- BRN approval/provider metadata is available

Certificate language must remain disabled or placeholder-only while approval is pending. After approval, provider-specific certificate text must be reviewed against the issued BRN provider information before use.

## Certificate Evidence

Retain Moodle evidence for completion and certificate release, including activity completion logs, assignment submissions, quiz attempts, capstone review status, final assessment result, attestation timestamps, and certificate issuance records.

## Compliance Guardrails

- Status remains Draft / Pending BRN CEP Approval until approval is issued.
- Use contact hours as the primary measurement.
- This course package is measured in contact hours, not CEUs.
- Do not claim BRN approval until provider approval is issued.
- Do not add government forms or signer binder materials to the Moodle content folder.
- Do not distribute blank certificates.
- Configure certificate release only after provider metadata is available and compliance review is complete.
