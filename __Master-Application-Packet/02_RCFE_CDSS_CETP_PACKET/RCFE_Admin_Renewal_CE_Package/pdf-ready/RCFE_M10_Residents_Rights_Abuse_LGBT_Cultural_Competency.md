## Module description

Live/instructor-interactive module on residents' rights, abuse and neglect prevention and mandated reporting, LGBT cultural competency (per California RCFE requirements), and complaint-safe, non-retaliatory communication. Delivered live for sensitive role-play and facilitated discussion.

## Measurable learning objectives

- Enumerate core residents' rights and the administrator's duty to uphold them.
- Identify signs of abuse/neglect and execute mandated-reporting obligations and timelines.
- Apply LGBT-inclusive, culturally competent practices required for California RCFEs.
- Respond to a complaint in a complaint-safe, non-retaliatory manner.
- Demonstrate, in live role-play, a rights-respecting response to a sensitive situation.

## Required readings / learning content sections

- **M10-L1 - Residents' Rights and Administrator Duties (Facilitated)** (55 min): Dignity, privacy, self-determination, freedom from restraint/retaliation, councils, grievance rights.
- **M10-L2 - Abuse/Neglect Recognition and Mandated Reporting** (55 min): Abuse types, mandated-reporter duties, reporting timelines/recipients, non-retaliation, documentation.
- **M10-L3 - LGBT Cultural Competency and Complaint-Safe Communication** (70 min): LGBT inclusive policies/forms/training, anti-discrimination, complaint handling without retaliation.
