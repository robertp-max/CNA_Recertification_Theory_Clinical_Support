## Module description

Self-paced module on RCFE business operations: required business and resident records, fiscal accountability, admission agreements, fee disclosures, insurance/bonding, claim-safe communications, and the administrator's personal accountability for accurate recordkeeping and truthful representations.

## Measurable learning objectives

- List the categories of business and resident records an RCFE must maintain and the minimum retention periods.
- Explain the administrator's fiscal accountability and bonding/insurance obligations.
- Identify required elements of an RCFE admission agreement and fee disclosure.
- Given a communication scenario, rewrite a statement to be accurate and claim-safe.
- Construct a records-retention schedule for a fictional facility.

## Required readings / learning content sections

- **M02-L1 - Required Records and Retention** (55 min): Resident records, personnel records, fiscal records, incident logs; retention minimums; storage and confidentiality.
- **M02-L2 - Admission Agreements, Fees, and Disclosures** (55 min): Required agreement terms, rate-change notice, refund handling, third-party services, prohibited terms.
- **M02-L3 - Accountability and Claim-Safe Communication** (70 min): Truthful marketing, avoiding overclaiming services, accurate occupancy/financial reporting, falsification consequences.
