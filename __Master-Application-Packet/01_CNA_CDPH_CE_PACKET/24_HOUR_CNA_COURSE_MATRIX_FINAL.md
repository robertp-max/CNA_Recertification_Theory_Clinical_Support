# 24-Hour CNA Course Matrix (Final)

Status: Draft / Pending Approval. Online CE only; no clinical hours; no hands-on competency validation. This online CE course does not, by itself, complete all California CNA renewal requirements. Fictional, de-identified resident examples only; no PHI.

Aligned course structure: **12 units x 2 online CE hours = 24 online CE hours total** (Year 1 = 12 hours, Year 2 = 12 hours).

Source backbone: CCCCO Nurse Assistant Model Curriculum (Revised December 2018), **Modules 10-17**. Each unit below records its source module. Source module numbers/titles are preserved as the content backbone; the learner-facing build, application packet, and CDPH course attachment all use this single 12-unit x 2-hour structure.

Documentation-ready posture: Vanessa Valerio is assigned as SME/instructor of record for this packet under the documentation-readiness override. Vanessa Valerio SME/instructor evidence (resume/CV, license verification, expiration date) remains a final verification attachment.
