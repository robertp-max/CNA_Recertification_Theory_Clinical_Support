# Generated No-PHI Learner Warnings

Status: Draft / Pending Approval. Generated Moodle MVP implementation artifact. Privacy/compliance review required.

## Short banner (top of every open-text/upload activity)
"Do not enter real patient or resident information. Use only fictional, de-identified examples. No PHI."

## Standard warning block (course intro + before scenario/documentation activities)
"This course must not contain protected health information (PHI). Do not enter real names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, addresses, photos, or any detail that could identify a real patient or resident. All scenarios are fictional. If you are unsure whether something is PHI, do not enter it. Any PHI entered may be removed and handled under facility privacy procedures."

## Upload control text (if uploads enabled)
"Do not upload real charts, photographs, or documents containing patient/resident information. Uploads should be limited to approved, de-identified, fictional materials only."

## Inline reminder (open-text question stems)
"(Reminder: fictional examples only - no PHI.)"

## Escalation note (instructor/admin facing)
"If PHI is detected in any submission, follow GENERATED_REMEDIATION_RULES and 16_NO_PHI_AND_PRIVACY_CONTROLS: quarantine the submission, remove PHI, document the incident, and notify the privacy contact."
