# Generated Attestation Texts

Status: Draft / Pending Approval. Generated Moodle MVP implementation artifact. Legal/compliance review required before production use. Implement as Choice/Feedback activities requiring submission.

## Identity confirmation (course start)
"I confirm that I am the person enrolled in this course and that I am personally completing all activities and assessments. I understand that providing false identity information may invalidate my completion record."

## No-PHI acknowledgment (before open-text/submission activities)
"I understand that I must not enter any protected health information (PHI) or any real patient/resident identifiers in this course. I will use only fictional, de-identified examples. I understand that any PHI I enter may be removed and reported per facility privacy procedures."

## Academic integrity attestation
"I attest that my responses are my own work and that I have not shared or received unauthorized assistance or answer keys for the assessments in this course."

## Unit/Module completion attestation
"I attest that I have personally reviewed the content and completed the activities for this [unit/module], and that the information I submitted is truthful to the best of my knowledge."

## CNA course completion attestation (online CE posture)
"I understand this is an online continuing-education course providing online CE hours only. It does not include clinical hours or hands-on competency validation and does not, by itself, complete all California CNA renewal requirements. Status: Draft / Pending Approval."

## RCFE course completion attestation
"I attest that I personally completed this RCFE continuing-education refresher course. I understand this is a renewal-level CE course and not initial certification. Status: Draft / Pending Approval."

## BRN course completion attestation (contact hours)
"I attest that I personally completed this RN case-management continuing-education course measured in contact hours (not CEUs). I understand this course makes no certification or scope-of-practice expansion claim. Status: Pending BRN CEP approval."

## Pre-approval certificate notice (shown wherever a certificate would appear)
"No continuing-education certificate is issued for this course until regulatory approval is granted. Any sample certificate is marked NOT VALID FOR CE CREDIT."
