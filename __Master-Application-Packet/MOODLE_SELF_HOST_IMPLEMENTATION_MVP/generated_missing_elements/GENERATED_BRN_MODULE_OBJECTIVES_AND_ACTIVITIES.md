# Generated BRN Module Objectives and Activities

Status: Pending BRN CEP approval. Generated Moodle MVP implementation artifact. SME review required. 30 contact hours = 10 modules x 3 contact hours; contact hours (not CEUs); provider approval; no certification/scope-expansion claim; no 4-contact-hour language.

Each module uses: Objectives, Timed Content (>=150 min), Case Scenario, Documentation/Decision Activity, Module Quiz (see shell spec).

## M01 - Foundations of RN Case Management and Nursing Scope
Objectives: define case-management purpose; practice within RN scope; apply patient-centered, ethical, collaborative foundations. Quiz: BRN/M01.

## M02 - Assessment, Intake, and Patient-Centered Care Planning
Objectives: conduct comprehensive assessment; reconcile intake; write measurable patient-centered goals; reassess. Quiz: BRN/M02.

## M03 - Care Coordination Across Disciplines and Settings
Objectives: coordinate the interdisciplinary team; use structured communication/handoffs; ensure cross-setting continuity. Quiz: BRN/M03.

## M04 - Documentation, Legal Charting, and Audit-Ready Records
Objectives: produce accurate/objective/timely documentation; correct errors properly; maintain confidentiality and audit readiness. Quiz: BRN/M04.

## M05 - Patient Rights, Consent, Advocacy, and Ethical Boundaries
Objectives: apply informed consent; respect autonomy and the right to refuse; maintain professional boundaries; use ethical decision-making. Quiz: BRN/M05. Legal items flagged for SME/legal review.

## M06 - Transitions of Care, Discharge Planning, Community Resources
Objectives: plan safe transitions; use teach-back and medication reconciliation; link community resources; reduce readmissions. Quiz: BRN/M06.

## M07 - Utilization Review, Medical Necessity, Payer-Safe Communication
Objectives: define utilization review/medical necessity; communicate truthfully (payer-safe); document necessity; balance advocacy and stewardship. Quiz: BRN/M07. Payer/legal items flagged for SME/legal review.

## M08 - Quality Assurance, Risk Management, Incident Reporting, Escalation
Objectives: apply QA/QI and risk management; complete objective incident/near-miss reports; escalate appropriately; support a just culture. Quiz: BRN/M08.

## M09 - Chronic Disease, Geriatric, and High-Risk Patient Management
Objectives: manage chronic/geriatric/high-risk patients; recognize early decompensation; address social determinants; coordinate complex care. Quiz: BRN/M09. Clinical thresholds flagged for SME review.

## M10 - Integrated Case Management Capstone
Objectives: integrate the full process across realistic scenarios safely, ethically, and within scope; apply reflective practice. Quiz: BRN/M10.

## CAPSTONE_FINAL - Comprehensive Capstone Final Assessment
Objectives: demonstrate integrated competency across all modules. Activity: capstone final (BRN/CAPSTONE_FINAL, 30 items), gated behind all 10 modules.
