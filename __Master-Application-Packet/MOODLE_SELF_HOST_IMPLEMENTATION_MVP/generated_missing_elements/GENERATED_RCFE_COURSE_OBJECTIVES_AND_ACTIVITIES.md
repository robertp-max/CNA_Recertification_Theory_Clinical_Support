# Generated RCFE Course Objectives and Activities

Status: Draft / Pending Approval. Generated Moodle MVP implementation artifact. SME review required. Renewal-level CE refresher for RCFE administrators; not initial certification. Filed 9-course/27-hour structure controlling. Regulatory specifics flagged for SME/legal review.

## RCFE-CETP-001 - Laws, Regulations, Policies, Procedural Standards (4h)
Objectives: identify the regulatory framework governing RCFEs; maintain current policies/records; cooperate with licensing; apply corrective action. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-001).

## RCFE-CETP-002 - Dementia: Person-Centered Care Foundations (4h)
Objectives: define person-centered dementia care; apply communication and behavioral-support strategies; engage families. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-002). Dementia-focused.

## RCFE-CETP-003 - Dementia: Safety, Environment, Documentation (4h)
Objectives: address wandering/elopement and fall risk; design supportive environments; document objectively; apply least-restrictive practices. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-003). Dementia-focused.

## RCFE-CETP-004 - Resident Rights, Dignity, Councils, Abuse Prevention (2h)
Objectives: identify resident rights; support resident councils; preserve dignity/privacy; prevent and respond to abuse. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-004).

## RCFE-CETP-005 - Medication Management in RCFE Operations (4h)
Objectives: apply assistance-with-self-administration limits; ensure secure storage/labeling/records; respond to errors; oversee within scope. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-005). Oversight scope only; not administration certification.

## RCFE-CETP-006 - Admission, Retention, Reappraisal, Needs and Services Plans (3h)
Objectives: conduct pre-admission appraisal; build/update needs and services plans; apply retention criteria; manage changing needs. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-006).

## RCFE-CETP-007 - Emergency Procedures and Physical Environment Controls (2h)
Objectives: maintain emergency/disaster plans and drills; ensure environmental safety; plan for residents needing assistance; conduct after-action review. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-007).

## RCFE-CETP-008 - Staff Supervision, Training Records, Administrator Accountability (2h)
Objectives: supervise staff effectively; maintain training records; ensure required training; apply accountability and corrective supervision. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-008).

## RCFE-CETP-009 - Business Operations, Records, Claim-Safe Communications (2h)
Objectives: maintain accurate records and retention; apply claim-safe communication; protect resident funds/confidentiality; cooperate with inspections. Activities: standard set; final (RCFE/RCFE-CETP-009).
