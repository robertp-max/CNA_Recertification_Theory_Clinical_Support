Module 2 assessment gaps to consider:

- Resident rights content is not directly assessed beyond abuse/reporting.
- Ombudsman/resident council protections are formative only.
- DNR/advance directive content is not assessed and needs scope review before any graded question.
- Physical, sexual, financial abuse, involuntary seclusion, abandonment, and healthcare fraud are not each independently assessed in the five-question quiz.
- HIPAA/confidentiality in abuse reporting is not assessed.
