Estimated time: 120 minutes. Required. Certificate gate: yes for theory completion, with quiz pass threshold of 80%.

### Suggested V2 Card Flow
- `modules.m4.overview` - Module overview: mobility, falls, body mechanics, emergency procedures, and CE boundary reminder.
- `modules.m4.l1.c01` - Concept: body mechanics principles - wide base, knees/hips, load close, core, pivot, push, get help.
- `modules.m4.l1.c02` - Quick check: safe lifting technique.
- `modules.m4.l2.c01` - Procedure: transfer safety setup - care plan, wheels locked, gait belt, clear path, count together.
- `modules.m4.l2.c02` - Procedure compare: bed-to-wheelchair, stand-pivot, slide board, mechanical lift.
- `modules.m4.l2.c03` - Concept: ambulation with gait belt, walker, and cane.
- `modules.m4.l2.c04` - Scenario: two-person transfer required; wait for help.
- `modules.m4.l3.c01` - Concept: AROM, AAROM, PROM, contracture prevention.
- `modules.m4.l3.c02` - Concept: restorative care and CNA support for independence.
- `modules.m4.l3.c03` - Quick check: adaptive spoon/self-feeding program.
- `modules.m4.l4.c01` - Concept: resident and environmental fall risk factors.
- `modules.m4.l4.c02` - Procedure: fall prevention responsibilities.
- `modules.m4.l4.c03` - Procedure: what to do after a fall.
- `modules.m4.l4.c04` - Multi-select check: fall prevention strategies.
- `modules.m4.l5.c01` - Concept: RACE and PASS fire safety.
- `modules.m4.l5.c02` - Procedure: conscious and unconscious choking response.
- `modules.m4.l5.c03` - Concept: disaster preparedness and facility plan.
- `modules.m4.quiz` - Graded module knowledge check.

### Assessment Coverage
Q4.1 body mechanics; Q4.2 transfer care plan; Q4.3 PROM safety; Q4.4 RACE fire safety; Q4.5 fall prevention and CNA scope.
