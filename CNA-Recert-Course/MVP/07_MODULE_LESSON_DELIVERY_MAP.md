Derived from `Content/02_THEORY_SYLLABUS_TABLE.md`, the full module files, the Async Blueprint's topic recommendations, and the Interaction Design Matrix. ## Module 7 — Review, Final Exam/Test & Affidavit
- **Purpose:** Cross-module review, summative exam, attestation. **Gates certificate release.**
- **Estimated duration:** 0.5 hr
- **Best delivery:** ungraded practice quiz + case-based review + **native randomized exam** (25 of 50, 80%) + affidavit. **Completion rule:** exam ≥80% + affidavit complete. **Remediation:** failed exam → locked period + review failed modules + randomized retake; **no answer-key reveal**. **Flag:** e-signature method unresolved (wet-sign fallback); certificate disabled until provider metadata + wording approved.

| Step | Purpose | Delivery | Interaction | Assessment | Job aid | UI |
