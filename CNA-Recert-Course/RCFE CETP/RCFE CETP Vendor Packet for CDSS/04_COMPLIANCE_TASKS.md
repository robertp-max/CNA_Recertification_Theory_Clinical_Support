# Compliance Tasks

### Compliance

PROVIDED:

- Current CDSS source research index.
- Official form inventory and filled draft set.
- Risk register and QA checklist.
- Final approval gate language.

ACTION STEPS:

- Check every official form against the CDSS Certification Forms page immediately before submission.
- Confirm all pending fields with source-backed documentation.
- Review LIC 9140 attachments against the ACB course-review guide.
- Confirm no approval claims appear on website/catalog materials.
- Prepare incomplete-notice response workflow before submission.

OUTPUT:

- Compliance-cleared CI Institute of Nursing RCFE CETP Approval Packet.

DO NOT:

- Do not submit incomplete forms when pending fields remain unresolved.
- Do not use unofficial form versions if CDSS updates the form page.
- Do not approve certificate issuance before CDSS approval.
