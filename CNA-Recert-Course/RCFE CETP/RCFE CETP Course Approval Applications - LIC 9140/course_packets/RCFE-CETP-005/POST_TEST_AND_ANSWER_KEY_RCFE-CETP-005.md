# Post-Test and Answer Key - RCFE-CETP-005

| No. | Question | Answer key |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | What is an administrator-level medication control? | Ensuring staff follow approved documentation, storage, communication, and escalation procedures. |
| 2 | What should occur when medication instructions are unclear? | Escalate for clarification through the approved physician/pharmacy communication process. |
| 3 | Why are medication refusals documented? | They affect resident safety, communication, follow-up, and regulatory review. |
| 4 | What should a medication audit check include? | Orders, labels, logs, storage, disposal, staff training, and error follow-up. |
| 5 | Which Core category applies? | Medication Management. |
