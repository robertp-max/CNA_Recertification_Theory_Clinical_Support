# LIC 9141 Application Tasks

        ## Step-by-Step Vendor Application Process

        1. Confirm legal entity and authority to conduct business.
2. Confirm authorized representative and signer.
3. Gather corporate documentation.
4. Complete LIC 9141.
5. Complete vendor fee notice or Vendor Automation Platform payment process.
6. Assemble vendor application appendices.
7. Run QA check.
8. Obtain Compliance clearance.
9. Obtain Dee approval.
10. Submit through Vendor Automation Platform or mail.
11. Track CDSS response.
12. Cure incomplete notice within deadline if needed.
13. Record vendor number only after issued.
