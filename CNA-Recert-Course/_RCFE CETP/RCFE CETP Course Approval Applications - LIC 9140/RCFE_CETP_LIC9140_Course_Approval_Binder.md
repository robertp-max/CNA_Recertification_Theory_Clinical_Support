# LIC 9140 Application Tasks

## Step-by-Step Course Approval Process

1. Confirm first-wave course list.
2. Confirm each course fits one RCFE Core of Knowledge category.
3. Complete one LIC 9140 per course.
4. Attach course description, objectives, outline, teaching methods, sources, evaluation method, participant assessment, active participation method, records statement, and location/reviewer access.
5. Attach instructor qualifications and disclosures.
6. Complete course fee notice or Vendor Automation Platform payment process.
7. Assemble each course packet.
8. Run course-level QA.
9. Obtain Compliance clearance.
10. Obtain Dee approval.
11. Submit through Vendor Automation Platform or mail.
12. Track CDSS response.
13. Cure incomplete notice within deadline if needed.
14. Record course numbers only after issued.
15. Activate post-approval roster, certificate, recordkeeping, and schedule controls.

# Course Approval Final Approval Gate

Execution may continue.

Do NOT proceed with the following until final clearance:

- Submit LIC 9140 applications to CDSS.
- Sign CDSS course forms.
- Pay course fees.
- Publish course approval claims.
