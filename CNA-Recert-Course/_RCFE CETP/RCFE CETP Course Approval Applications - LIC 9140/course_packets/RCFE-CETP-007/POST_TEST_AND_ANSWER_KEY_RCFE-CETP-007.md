# Post-Test and Answer Key - RCFE-CETP-007

| No. | Question | Answer key |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Why are clear passageways an administrator concern? | They affect resident safety, emergency movement, and regulatory compliance. |
| 2 | What should happen when an environmental hazard is identified? | Document, correct or mitigate, communicate, and escalate when risk remains. |
| 3 | What is a disaster-plan readiness control? | Maintaining current procedures, staff awareness, drills, supplies, and communication plans. |
| 4 | Why are hazardous materials storage controls important? | They reduce resident access risk and support safe operations. |
| 5 | Which Core category applies? | Managing the Physical Environment. |
