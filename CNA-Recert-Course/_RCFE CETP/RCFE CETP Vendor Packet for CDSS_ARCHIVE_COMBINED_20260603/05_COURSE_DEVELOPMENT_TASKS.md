### Course Development

PROVIDED:

- Detailed course packets in Appendix B.
- LIC 9140 filled drafts for each proposed course.
- Post-test and answer key for each proposed course.

ACTION STEPS:

- Review and approve first-wave course list.
- Confirm course materials are consistent with each approved outline.
- Assign instructors by segment and attach qualification evidence.
- Confirm LMS/live-stream participation controls before submission.

OUTPUT:

- Submission-ready course approval packets.

DO NOT:

- Do not leave any LIC 9140 attachment as generic instructions.
- Do not map one CETP course to multiple Core categories.
- Do not issue certificates before approval.
