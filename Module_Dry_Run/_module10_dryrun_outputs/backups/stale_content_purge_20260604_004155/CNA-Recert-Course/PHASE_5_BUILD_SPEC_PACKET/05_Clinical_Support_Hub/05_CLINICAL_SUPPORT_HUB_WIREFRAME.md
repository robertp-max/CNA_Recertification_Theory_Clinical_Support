# Optional Clinical Support Hub Wireframe

**Purpose:** Text-based wireframe for the integrated 12-hour clinical practice support pathway.  
**Hard rule:** Use "optional clinical support," "skills refresh," "practice support," or "documentation support." Do not imply required California renewal clinical hours.

---

## Hub Landing Page

### Page Title

`Optional Clinical Support Hub`

### Subtitle

`Skills refresh, practice planning, scheduling guidance, and documentation support`

### Top Banner Copy

This hub is here to help you prepare, organize practice support, and keep non-PHI documentation in one place. It is separate from your online CE certificate requirements unless CI Institute of Nursing gives you written CDPH-approved instructions.

---

## Section 1: Start Here - What This Is / What This Is Not

### Wireframe

- Short overview.
- Clinical support disclaimer.
- Button: `Open Skills Refresh Library`.
- Button: `Plan Optional Practice Support`.
- Button: `Read What Not to Upload`.

### Clinical Support Disclaimer

> This Clinical Support Hub provides optional clinical support, skills refresh, scheduling/resource support, and documentation support. These activities are not California CNA renewal clinical hours and do not replace CDPH renewal documentation unless CDPH approves that use in writing. Skipping optional clinical support does not block your online CE certificate if you complete all required online CE certificate steps.

Status: Ready for compliance review; needs legal/CDPH review before learner release.

## Section 7: Documentation Support

### Wireframe

- When to use this area.
- What to upload.
- What not to upload.
- Non-PHI attestation checkbox.
- Upload activity.
- Resubmission instructions.

### Documentation Upload Warning

> Use this upload only for non-PHI documentation requested by CI Institute of Nursing. Do not upload patient/resident records, chart screenshots, medication records, faces, names, dates of birth, addresses, medical record numbers, or any detail that could identify a patient or resident. If your file contains patient/resident identifiers, do not upload it.

Status: Needs privacy review and legal review.

## Section 8: RN/Preceptor Signoff Instructions

### Wireframe

- Explain when signoff may be useful.
- State that signoff is documentation support only unless CDPH approves credit.
- Downloadable signoff form.
- Required fields:
  - Learner name.
  - Date.
  - Setting type, not patient/resident details.
  - RN/preceptor name.
  - Title/role.
  - License/credential if applicable.
  - Employer/site.
  - Contact information.
  - Signature.
  - Non-PHI attestation.

### Build Note

Use a downloadable form plus Assignment upload for MVP. Do not enable certificate gate from signoff upload.

---

## Section 9: What Not to Upload

### PHI Warning

> Do not upload or type patient/resident names, faces, medical record numbers, dates of birth, addresses, chart screenshots, medication records, facility records, or any information that could identify a patient or resident. Use only de-identified learning documentation requested by the course. Submissions containing patient/resident identifiers may be rejected, deleted, or escalated according to privacy procedures.

Status: Needs privacy review.

## Section 10: Simulation Disclaimer

> Online scenarios, virtual simulations, LTI simulations, and practice activities are learning supports only. They do not count as clinical hours or hands-on competency verification for California CNA renewal unless CDPH provides written approval for that specific use.
