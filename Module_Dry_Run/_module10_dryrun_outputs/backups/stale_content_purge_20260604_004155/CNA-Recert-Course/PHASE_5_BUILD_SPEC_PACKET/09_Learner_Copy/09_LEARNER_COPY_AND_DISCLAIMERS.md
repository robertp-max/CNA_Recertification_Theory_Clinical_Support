## Orientation Page

**Draft copy:**

Welcome. This course is designed to help you complete 12 hours of online CNA continuing education through Moodle, if CI Institute of Nursing and this course are approved for California CNA online CE.

You will move through short lessons, practice checks, required interactions, a final exam/test, and a final statement before your certificate can be released. You can stop and come back. Your progress is saved.

This course does not complete all California CNA renewal requirements by itself. California CNA renewal requires 48 hours over each two-year certification period, with at least 12 hours completed each year. Only 24 of the 48 hours may be completed online through CDPH-approved online CE.

## Certificate Release Reminder

**Draft copy:**

Your online CE certificate can be released only after all required certificate steps are complete:

- Your legal name and CNA certificate number are entered.
- You complete the online CE limit acknowledgement.
- You complete all required theory activities.
- You meet the active participation time requirement.
- You complete required interactions and feedback activities.
- You pass the final exam/test.
- You complete the final signed statement/affidavit.
- Required certificate fields are available.

Optional clinical support activities do not block this online CE certificate unless CI Institute of Nursing gives you written CDPH-approved instructions.

**Draft copy:**
